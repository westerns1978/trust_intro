Pillar 1 - Authentic

First, Authentic. Imagine giving every document a digital birth certificate. Using cutting-edge C2PA technology, we embed verifiable metadata directly into your documents at the moment of creation. This isn't just a timestamp; it's a digital fingerprint that proves its origin and prevents tampering. What you see is genuinely what was created – no doubts, no fakes.

Pillar 2 - Verified

Next, Verified. Access to sensitive information demands robust security. Our iVALT Multi-Factor Authentication system provides unparalleled protection with up to 25 authentication points. From advanced biometrics to secure device identification, iVALT ensures that only authorized users can access your critical documents. This significantly enhances your security posture and simplifies compliance.

Pillar 3 - Immutable

And finally, Immutable. With Barlea blockchain technology, we create tamper-proof audit trails for every single action taken on a document. Think of it as an unbreakable chain of evidence. Every view, every edit, every share is recorded on a distributed ledger that cannot be altered. This provides unalterable records crucial for compliance, dispute resolution, and absolute accountability.
